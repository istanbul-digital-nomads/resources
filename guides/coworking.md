# Coworking Spaces in Istanbul

## Popular Spaces

### Workinton
Multiple locations across Istanbul (Levent, Maslak, Atasehir, Kadikoy). Professional environment with meeting rooms, phone booths, and events. Day passes available.

- **Price:** Day pass from 250 TL, monthly from 3,500 TL
- **Wifi:** 100+ Mbps
- **Website:** workinton.com

### Kolektif House
Locations in Levent, Sariyer, and Maslak. Modern spaces with a strong community focus. Regular networking events and workshops.

- **Price:** Day pass from 300 TL, monthly from 4,000 TL
- **Wifi:** 100+ Mbps
- **Website:** kolektifhouse.co

### Impact Hub Istanbul
Community-focused space in Cihangir/Beyoglu area. Good for social entrepreneurs and people working on impact-driven projects.

- **Price:** Monthly from 3,000 TL
- **Wifi:** Reliable
- **Website:** istanbul.impacthub.net

### Daire Coworking
Smaller, cozier space in Kadikoy. Feels more like a shared office than a corporate coworking brand. Good community.

- **Price:** Monthly from 2,500 TL
- **Wifi:** Solid
- **Location:** Kadikoy

### Salt Galata
Not a traditional coworking space but a free library and research center in a stunning historic building in Karakoy. Great for a few hours of focused work.

- **Price:** Free
- **Wifi:** Available
- **Note:** Can get crowded, no guaranteed seating

## Tips

- Many coworking spaces offer free trial days. Always try before you commit.
- If you only need a desk a few days a week, cafe-hopping might be more cost-effective.
- Ask about community events. The best coworking spaces are also social hubs.
- Check if 24/7 access is included in your plan if you work odd hours.
