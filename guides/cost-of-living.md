# Cost of Living in Istanbul (2026)

## Monthly Budget Breakdown

### Budget Nomad (15,000 to 25,000 TL/month)

| Category | Estimated Cost |
|----------|---------------|
| Rent (shared apartment) | 8,000 to 12,000 TL |
| Food (mostly cooking) | 4,000 to 6,000 TL |
| Transport | 1,000 to 1,500 TL |
| Phone/Internet | 500 to 800 TL |
| Other | 1,500 to 4,700 TL |

## Money Tips

- The Turkish Lira fluctuates. Keep an eye on exchange rates if you earn in foreign currency.
- Use Wise or Revolut for currency conversion. Better rates than local exchange offices.
- Credit cards are widely accepted but carry cash for small shops and markets.
- Tipping: 5 to 10% at restaurants is common. Round up for taxis and delivery.
