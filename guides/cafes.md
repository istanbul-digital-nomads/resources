# Laptop-Friendly Cafes in Istanbul

## Kadikoy / Moda

### MOC (Ministry of Coffee)
A nomad favorite. Spacious, good wifi, plenty of outlets, and they don't mind you staying for hours.
- **Wifi:** 50+ Mbps
- **Outlets:** Yes
- **Address:** Caferaga Mah, Kadikoy

### Montag Coffee Roasters
Specialty coffee with a clean, minimal interior. Good for focused work.
- **Wifi:** Good
- **Outlets:** Limited
- **Address:** Moda, Kadikoy

### Walter's Coffee Roastery
Breaking Bad themed cafe with solid wifi and a work-friendly atmosphere.
- **Wifi:** Good
- **Outlets:** Yes
- **Address:** Caferaga Mah, Kadikoy

## Besiktas

### Kronotrop Coffee
Excellent specialty coffee, reliable wifi. Multiple Istanbul locations.
- **Wifi:** 40+ Mbps
- **Outlets:** Some tables
- **Address:** Besiktas (also in Cihangir and Levent)

## Cihangir / Beyoglu

### Norm Coffee
Bright, airy space popular with freelancers. Good pastries.
- **Wifi:** Reliable
- **Outlets:** Yes
- **Address:** Cihangir

### Coffee Department
Cozy spot with consistent wifi. Can get busy on weekends.
- **Wifi:** Good
- **Outlets:** Yes
- **Address:** Cihangir

## Karakoy

### Karabatak
Hidden courtyard cafe with a creative vibe. Good for a few hours of work.
- **Wifi:** Decent
- **Outlets:** Limited
- **Address:** Karakoy

## Tips for Cafe Working

- Always buy something every couple of hours. It's respectful to the business.
- Test the wifi before settling in. Some cafes advertise wifi but the speed is unusable.
- Bring a portable charger as a backup.
- Avoid peak lunch hours (12:00 to 14:00) at smaller cafes.
- Turkish tea (cay) is cheap and endless refills are common. Great for long work sessions.
